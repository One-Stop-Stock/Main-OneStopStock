What is One-Stop-Stock?

One-Stop-Stock aims to streamline anyones electronic shopping experience!

Do you prefer the convenience of shopping online and picking up your order at a nearby store? Do you ever find yourself endlessly browsing online to find a nearby store that has a specific item you are looking for? The main goal of One-Stop-Stock is to give online shoppers an easy and convenient way to shop online and find the exact product they are looking for in their area.
